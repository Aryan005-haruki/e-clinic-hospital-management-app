# Hospital App - Direct Access Mode (No Authentication Required!)

## ✅ APP AB BINA AUTHENTICATION KE CHALEGA!

### 🎯 KAISE USE KAREIN:

**Step 1: App Open Karein**
- App open hone par "Welcome To Tatwa Digital Health Care" screen dikhega
- 2 options honge: **Doctor** aur **Patient**

**Step 2: Select Karein**
- **Doctor** ke liye: "Doctor" button click karein
- **Patient** ke liye: "Patient" button click karein

**Step 3: Continue Click Karein**
- Bas "Continue" button dabayein
- **SEEDHA DASHBOARD KHUL JAYEGA!** ✅
- **NO PASSWORD, NO OTP, NO VERIFICATION!** 🎉
